About the Derby

The Duxford Soapbox Derby was established in 2013 to raise money for Cancer Research UK in memory of Phill Hill. It's a wonderful chaotic dash around the village by would be grand prix stars racing in anything from a wheelbarrow to an old iron bath.

Taking part

Entries to the cart race are limited and will be allocated on a first come, first served basis. Applications must be received by 31st August 2019.
